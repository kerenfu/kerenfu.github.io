A Little Bit Of My Story

I received the B.S. degree from Huazhong University of Science and Technology, Wuhan, China, in 2011, and dual Ph.D. degrees from Shanghai Jiao Tong University, Shanghai, China, and Chalmers University of Technology, Gothenburg, Sweden, in 2016, under the joint supervision of Prof. Jie Yang and Prof. Irene Yu-Hua Gu. I am currently a research associated professor with College of Computer Science, Sichuan University, Chengdu, China. My current research interests include visual computing, saliency analysis, and machine learning.
